About Us

Founded in 2021 as IASA and incorporated in 2024 as ASTR Int’l, we are a victim-centered nonprofit combating scams and trafficking.

Mission & Vision

  • Prevent and combat cross-border fraud and trafficking
  • Provide safe, accessible and trusted victim support
  • Advance systemic change through data and advocacy
