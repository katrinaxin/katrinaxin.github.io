About ASTR Int’l

ASTR Int’l (Anti Scam & Trafficking Rescue International) is a registered non-governmental organization (NGO) based in Texas, USA, dedicated to combating fraud and human trafficking on both local and global scales. Originally founded in 2021 by volunteers under the name IASA (International Anti-Scam and Human Trafficking Alliance), the organization was formally incorporated as ASTR Int’l in 2024.

Our mission extends beyond raising awareness—we are committed to taking concrete and impactful actions that bring meaningful change to the lives of victims.

Core Initiatives

  1. Anti-Scam Awareness and Prevention — We research and raise awareness of rapidly evolving cyber scams, expose tactics and share case studies to prevent victimization, and provide tailored support for victims.
  2. Rescue and Rehabilitation of Coerced Victims — We assist individuals coerced into fraudulent operations through deception, abduction, or trafficking, including those forced into sexual exploitation, helping them escape and rebuild.
  3. Intelligence to Support Law Enforcement — We collaborate with partners and authorities worldwide to collect and share intelligence on scam syndicates to disrupt criminal networks.

关于 ASTR Int’l

ASTR Int’l（反诈反人口贩运援助国际组织） 是一家在美国德克萨斯州注册的非政府组织（NGO），致力于在本地及全球范围内打击诈骗和人口贩运。该组织由志愿者于 2021 年创立，前身为 IASA（反诈骗与人口贩卖联盟），并于 2024 年正式注册为 ASTR Int’l。

我们的使命不仅是提高公众意识，更是通过切实可行的行动，为受害者带来真正的改变与支持。

核心工作方向

  1. 反诈宣传与预防 —— 研究并揭露不断演变的网络诈骗手段，分享案例以减少受害；同时为受害者提供量身定制的支持。
  2. 被胁迫受害者的救援与康复 —— 援助因欺骗、绑架或人口贩卖而被迫参与诈骗活动的受害者，包括被迫从事性剥削者，帮助其脱困重建。
  3. 情报收集与执法协作 —— 与全球伙伴与执法机构合作，收集并共享涉诈犯罪情报，协助打击犯罪网络。
